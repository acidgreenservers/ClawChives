WHAT'S DIFFERENT NOW

Before (Manual Pattern):
  User clicks star
    ↓ handleToggleStar calls db.updateBookmark()
    ↓ await loadData() fetches ALL bookmarks
    ↓ Full dashboard re-renders (690+ items)
    ↓ 500-1000ms wait → VISIBLE FLICKER

After (React Query Pattern):
  User clicks star
    ↓ handleToggleStar calls updateBookmark()
    ↓ onMutate: Cache updated IMMEDIATELY
    ↓ Dashboard re-renders from cache
    ↓ 0-10ms wait → NO FLICKER (optimistic)
    ↓ Background: db.updateBookmark() silently completes
